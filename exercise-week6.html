Current Week: Week 6

Focus: Endurance Building

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with moderate hills and short jogs
  • Jogging with 15-second sprints every 3 minutes
  • Cycling (moderate-high pace with hills)
  • Swimming (moderate pace with interval laps)
  • Rowing machine (moderate-high resistance)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort, breathing should be labored but controlled

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill (incline walking or jogging with intervals)
  • Elliptical trainer (moderate-high resistance)
  • Stationary bike (interval program, moderate-high level)
  • Rowing machine (moderate-high resistance)
  • Stair climber (moderate-high pace)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort, breathing should be labored but controlled

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Strength Training - Upper Body (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with minimal rest between exercises:

  • Push-ups (standard or modified): 15 reps
  • Resistance band rows: 15 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands: 15 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair: 15 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands: 15 reps each arm
  • Plank: 75 seconds

Rest 30-45 seconds between sets

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with minimal rest between exercises:

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell): 15 reps
  • Seated rows: 15 reps
  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell): 15 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns: 15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 15 reps each arm
  • Cable rotations: 15 each side

Rest 30-45 seconds between sets

Day 3: Rope Flow & Mobility (25-30 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Rope Flow (or substitute with resistance band if no rope):

  • Basic figure 8 pattern: 90 seconds
  • Alternating side swings: 90 seconds
  • Overhead circles: 90 seconds
  • Figure 8 to side swing combo: 90 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 2 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Rope Flow (using battle ropes if available):

  • Alternating waves: 45 seconds
  • Double arm waves: 45 seconds
  • Side-to-side waves: 45 seconds
  • In-and-out waves: 45 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 2 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Day 4: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one (different from Day 1):

  • Brisk walking with moderate hills and short jogs
  • Jogging with 15-second sprints every 3 minutes
  • Cycling (moderate-high pace with hills)
  • Swimming (moderate pace with interval laps)
  • Rowing machine (moderate-high resistance)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort, breathing should be labored but controlled

Duration: 25-30 minutes

  • Treadmill (incline walking or jogging with intervals)
  • Elliptical trainer (moderate-high resistance)

  • Rowing machine (moderate-high resistance)
  • Stair climber (moderate-high pace)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort, breathing should be labored but controlled

Day 5: Strength Training - Lower Body (Knee-Friendly) (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with minimal rest between exercises:

  • Bodyweight box squats (using chair): 15-20 reps
  • Glute bridges with 3-second hold at top: 15-20 reps
  • Standing calf raises (single leg): 15-20 reps each
  • Side-lying leg raises with ankle weight (if available): 15-20 reps each side
  • Step-ups (low height): 15-20 each leg
  • Bird-dog with 3-second hold: 15 each side

Rest 30-45 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with minimal rest between exercises:

  • Leg press (moderate weight, partial range): 15-20 reps
  • Hip abduction machine: 15-20 reps
  • Hip extension machine: 15-20 reps
  • Seated calf raises: 20 reps
  • Stability ball hamstring curls: 15-20 reps
  • Cable pull-throughs: 15-20 reps

Rest 30-45 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 10-12 rounds:

  • 30 seconds high intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, or burpees)
  • 45 seconds recovery (walking or light movement)

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 10-12 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 30 seconds high intensity
  • 45 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 6

  • Focus on endurance by reducing rest periods and increasing repetitions
  • Maintain proper form while increasing work capacity
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
