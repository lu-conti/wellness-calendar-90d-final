12-Week Exercise Program

Current Week: Week 3

Focus: Progressive Overload

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with short jogging intervals
  • Light jogging with brief faster intervals
  • Cycling (moderate pace with moderate hills)
  • Swimming (moderate pace with technique focus)
  • Rowing machine (moderate resistance)

Intensity: You should be able to speak in short sentences

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill (incline walking with moderate incline)
  • Elliptical trainer (moderate resistance)
  • Stationary bike (interval program, moderate level)
  • Rowing machine (moderate resistance)
  • Stair climber (moderate pace)

Intensity: You should be able to speak in short sentences

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Strength Training - Upper Body (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Push-ups (standard or modified): 12 reps
  • Resistance band rows: 15 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands: 12 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair: 12 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands: 12-15 reps each arm
  • Plank: 60 seconds

Rest 45-60 seconds between sets

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell): 12 reps
  • Seated rows: 15 reps
  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell): 12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns: 15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 12-15 reps each arm
  • Cable rotations: 15 each side

Rest 45-60 seconds between sets

Day 3: Rope Flow & Mobility (25-30 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 12 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 6 each side
  • Hip circles: 12 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 12 each direction

Rope Flow (or substitute with resistance band if no rope):

  • Basic figure 8 pattern: 1 minute
  • Alternating side swings: 1 minute
  • Overhead circles: 1 minute
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 3 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 12 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 6 each side
  • Hip circles: 12 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 12 each direction

Rope Flow (using battle ropes if available):

  • Alternating waves: 30 seconds
  • Double arm waves: 30 seconds
  • Side-to-side waves: 30 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 3 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Day 4: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one (different from Day 1):

  • Brisk walking with short jogging intervals
  • Light jogging with brief faster intervals
  • Cycling (moderate pace with moderate hills)
  • Swimming (moderate pace with technique focus)
  • Rowing machine (moderate resistance)

Intensity: You should be able to speak in short sentences

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one (different from Day 1):

  • Treadmill (incline walking with moderate incline)
  • Elliptical trainer (moderate resistance)
  • Stationary bike (interval program, moderate level)
  • Rowing machine (moderate resistance)
  • Stair climber (moderate pace)

Intensity: You should be able to speak in short sentences

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 5: Strength Training - Lower Body (Knee-Friendly) (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Bodyweight box squats (using chair): 12-15 reps
  • Glute bridges with 2-second hold at top: 15 reps
  • Standing calf raises (single leg): 15 reps each
  • Side-lying leg raises with ankle weight (if available): 15 reps each side
  • Step-ups (low height): 12-15 each leg
  • Bird-dog with 2-second hold: 12 each side

Rest 45-60 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Leg press (light-moderate weight, partial range): 12-15 reps
  • Hip abduction machine: 15-20 reps
  • Hip extension machine: 15-20 reps
  • Seated calf raises: 15-20 reps
  • Stability ball hamstring curls: 12-15 reps
  • Cable pull-throughs: 15 reps

Rest 45-60 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8-10 rounds:

  • 30 seconds high intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, or burpees)
  • 60 seconds recovery (walking or light movement)

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8-10 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 30 seconds high intensity
  • 60 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 3

  • Introducing Rope Flow for coordination and shoulder mobility
  • Increased intensity and reduced rest periods
  • Continue to focus on proper form for all exercises
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
