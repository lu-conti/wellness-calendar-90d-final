Focus: Integrated Movement Patterns

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with arm movements (arm circles, punches, etc.)
  • Jogging with skipping, high knees, and butt kicks
  • Cycling with upper body twists
  • Swimming with varied strokes and breathing patterns
  • Rowing machine with varied stroke rates

Intensity: Moderate effort with focus on whole-body integration

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Functional Strength - Integrated Patterns (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Squat to overhead press with resistance band: 12 reps
  • Lunge with bicep curl: 10 each leg
  • Push-up to side plank: 6 each side
  • Glute bridge with chest fly (resistance band): 12 reps
  • Bird-dog with row (resistance band): 10 each side
  • Standing wood chop (resistance band): 10 each side

Rest 45 seconds between sets

Focus on fluid transitions between movement patterns

Day 3: Animal Flow & Mobility (25-30 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Animal Flow Circuit (repeat 3 times):

  • Beast to crab flow: 8 repetitions
  • Side kickthrough flow: 8 each side
  • Ape to beast to bear flow: 8 repetitions
  • Forward/backward/lateral beast crawl: 40 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between circuits

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 4 rounds of this circuit:

  • Jumping jacks: 30 seconds
  • Push-ups (modified if needed): 30 seconds
  • Bodyweight squats: 30 seconds
  • Mountain climbers: 30 seconds
  • Rest 60 seconds between rounds

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Complete 4 rounds of this circuit:

  • Rowing machine sprint: 30 seconds
  • Dumbbell push press: 30 seconds
  • Kettlebell swings: 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 30 seconds

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 11

  • Focus on integrated movement patterns that combine multiple muscle groups
  • Emphasis on multi-planar movement (sagittal, frontal, and transverse planes)
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
