Focus: Functional Movement Patterns

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with varied terrain
  • Jogging with directional changes (forward, side shuffle, backward)
  • Cycling with standing intervals
  • Swimming with varied strokes
  • Rowing machine with varied intensity

Intensity: Moderate effort with focus on movement quality

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill with varied incline and directional changes

  • Stationary bike with standing intervals

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Functional Strength - Push Patterns (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Push-ups (standard or modified): 12-15 reps
  • Standing resistance band chest press: 15 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands: 12-15 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair: 12-15 reps
  • Plank with shoulder taps: 20 taps total
  • Wall push (stand facing wall, fall forward and push back): 10 reps

Rest 45 seconds between sets

Focus on proper push mechanics and core engagement

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell): 12-15 reps

  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell): 12-15 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns: 15 reps
  • Medicine ball chest pass against wall: 15 reps
  • Plank with shoulder taps: 20 taps total

Rest 45 seconds between sets

Focus on proper push mechanics and core engagement

Day 3: Rope Flow & Mobility (25-30 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Rope Flow (or substitute with resistance band if no rope):

  • Basic figure 8 pattern: 60 seconds
  • Alternating side swings: 60 seconds
  • Overhead circles: 60 seconds
  • Figure 8 to side swing combo: 60 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 2 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Rope Flow (using battle ropes if available):

  • Alternating waves: 45 seconds
  • Double arm waves: 45 seconds
  • Side-to-side waves: 45 seconds
  • In-and-out waves: 45 seconds
  • Rest 30 seconds between each pattern
  • Repeat sequence 2 times

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Day 5: Functional Strength - Pull Patterns (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Resistance band rows: 15 reps
  • Resistance band pull-aparts: 15 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands: 15 reps each arm
  • Doorway rows (hold doorframe and lean back): 12 reps
  • Supermans: 15 reps with 2-second hold
  • Bird-dog with 3-second hold: 12 each side

Rest 45 seconds between sets

Focus on proper pull mechanics and back engagement

Complete 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Seated rows: 15 reps
  • Lat pulldowns: 15 reps
  • Face pulls: 15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 15 reps each arm
  • Reverse flys: 15 reps
  • Back extensions: 15 reps

Rest 45 seconds between sets

Focus on proper pull mechanics and back engagement

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8 rounds:

  • 20 seconds maximum intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, or burpees)
  • 10 seconds transition
  • 20 seconds maximum intensity (different exercise)
  • 10 seconds transition
  • 20 seconds maximum intensity (different exercise)
  • 60 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 20 seconds maximum intensity
  • 10 seconds transition
  • 20 seconds maximum intensity
  • 10 seconds transition
  • 20 seconds maximum intensity
  • 60 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 10

  • Focus on functional movement patterns (push/pull)
  • Emphasis on quality of movement over quantity
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
