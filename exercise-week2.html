Current Week: Week 2

Focus: Building Consistency

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with intervals of faster pace
  • Light jogging with walking recovery
  • Cycling (moderate pace with gentle hills)
  • Swimming (easy pace with occasional faster laps)
  • Rowing machine (varying resistance)

Intensity: You should be able to maintain a conversation

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill (incline walking with varied incline)
  • Elliptical trainer (varied resistance)
  • Stationary bike (interval program, easy level)
  • Rowing machine (varied resistance)
  • Stair climber (alternating between slow and moderate pace)

Intensity: You should be able to maintain a conversation

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Strength Training - Upper Body (25-30 minutes)

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Push-ups (modified if needed): 10-12 reps
  • Resistance band rows: 12-15 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands: 10-12 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair: 10-12 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands: 12 reps each arm
  • Plank: 45-60 seconds

Rest 60 seconds between sets

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell): 10-12 reps

  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell): 10-12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns: 12-15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 12 reps each arm
  • Cable rotations: 12 each side

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Day 3: Mobility & Animal Flow (25-30 minutes)

Complete the following sequence:

  • Cat-cow stretch: 12 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 6 each side
  • Hip circles: 12 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 12 each direction
  • Bear crawl: 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward
  • Crab walk: 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward
  • Lizard stretch: 40 seconds each side
  • Downward dog to cobra flow: 6 repetitions
  • Repeat sequence 2-3 times

Day 5: Strength Training - Lower Body (Knee-Friendly) (25-30 minutes)

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Bodyweight box squats (using chair): 12 reps
  • Glute bridges: 15 reps
  • Standing calf raises: 20 reps
  • Side-lying leg raises: 15 reps each side
  • Step-ups (low height): 12 each leg
  • Bird-dog: 12 each side

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 7-9 rounds:

  • 30 seconds high intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, or mountain climbers)
  • 90 seconds recovery (walking or light movement)

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 7-9 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 30 seconds high intensity
  • 90 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 2

  • Slightly increased intensity from Week 1
  • Continue to focus on proper form for all exercises
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
