Current Week: Week 7

Focus: Power Development

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking with steep hills and jogging intervals
  • Jogging with 20-second sprints every 2 minutes
  • Cycling (high pace with steep hills)
  • Swimming (moderate-high pace with sprint laps)
  • Rowing machine (high resistance)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort with power intervals

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill (incline jogging with sprint intervals)
  • Elliptical trainer (high resistance with sprint intervals)
  • Stationary bike (interval program, high level)
  • Rowing machine (high resistance with power strokes)
  • Stair climber (high pace with double-step intervals)

Intensity: Moderate-high effort with power intervals

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Strength Training - Upper Body (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with explosive concentric phase:

  • Push-ups with explosive up phase: 12 reps
  • Resistance band rows with explosive pull: 12 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands (explosive up): 12 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair with explosive up: 12 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands (explosive up): 12 reps each arm
  • Plank with shoulder taps: 20 taps total

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Focus on explosive power in the concentric (lifting) phase

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with explosive concentric phase:

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell) with explosive push: 12 reps
  • Seated rows with explosive pull: 12 reps
  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell) with explosive push: 12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns with explosive down: 12 reps
  • Bicep curls with explosive up: 12 reps each arm
  • Cable rotations with explosive twist: 12 each side

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Focus on explosive power in the concentric (lifting) phase

Day 3: Animal Flow & Mobility (25-30 minutes)

Mobility warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Cat-cow stretch: 15 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 8 each side
  • Hip circles: 15 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 15 each direction

Animal Flow Circuit (repeat 3 times):

  • Beast position hold: 30 seconds
  • Beast to crab transition with reach: 10 repetitions
  • Side kickthrough with hold: 8 each side
  • Ape to beast transition: 10 repetitions
  • Forward/backward beast crawl with power: 40 seconds
  • Rest 45 seconds between circuits

Cool down with gentle stretching (5 minutes)

Day 5: Strength Training - Lower Body (Knee-Friendly) (25-30 minutes)

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with explosive concentric phase:

  • Bodyweight box squats (using chair) with explosive up: 12-15 reps
  • Glute bridges with explosive up: 12-15 reps
  • Standing calf raises (single leg) with explosive up: 12-15 reps each
  • Side-lying leg raises with ankle weight (if available): 12-15 reps each side
  • Step-ups (low height) with explosive up: 12 each leg
  • Bird-dog with explosive extension: 12 each side

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on explosive power in the concentric phase while maintaining knee-friendly form

Complete 3 sets of each exercise with explosive concentric phase:

  • Leg press (moderate weight, partial range) with explosive push: 12-15 reps
  • Hip abduction machine with explosive out: 12-15 reps
  • Hip extension machine with explosive extension: 12-15 reps
  • Seated calf raises with explosive up: 15 reps
  • Stability ball hamstring curls with explosive curl: 12 reps
  • Cable pull-throughs with explosive hip thrust: 12-15 reps

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on explosive power in the concentric phase while maintaining knee-friendly form

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8-10 rounds:

  • 20 seconds maximum intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, or burpees)
  • 40 seconds recovery (walking or light movement)

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 8-10 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 20 seconds maximum intensity
  • 40 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 7

  • Focus on power development with explosive movements
  • Maintain proper form while incorporating power
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
