12-Week Exercise Program

Current Week: Week 1

Focus: Foundation Building

Day 1: Zone 2 Cardio (25-30 minutes)

Choose one:

  • Brisk walking
  • Light jogging
  • Cycling (moderate pace)
  • Swimming (easy pace)
  • Rowing machine (light resistance)

Intensity: You should be able to maintain a conversation

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Choose one:

  • Treadmill (incline walking)
  • Elliptical trainer
  • Stationary bike
  • Rowing machine
  • Stair climber (slow pace)

Intensity: You should be able to maintain a conversation

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 2: Strength Training - Upper Body (25-30 minutes)

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Push-ups (modified if needed): 8-12 reps
  • Resistance band rows: 12-15 reps
  • Shoulder press with resistance bands: 10-12 reps
  • Tricep dips on chair: 8-12 reps
  • Bicep curls with resistance bands: 10-12 reps each arm
  • Plank: 30-45 seconds

Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Chest press (machine or dumbbell): 10-12 reps
  • Seated rows: 12-15 reps
  • Shoulder press (machine or dumbbell): 10-12 reps
  • Tricep pushdowns: 12-15 reps
  • Bicep curls: 10-12 reps each arm
  • Cable rotations: 10 each side

Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Day 3: Mobility & Animal Flow (25-30 minutes)

Complete the following sequence:

  • Cat-cow stretch: 10 repetitions
  • World's greatest stretch: 5 each side
  • Hip circles: 10 each direction
  • Shoulder circles: 10 each direction
  • Bear crawl: 20 seconds forward, 20 seconds backward
  • Crab walk: 20 seconds forward, 20 seconds backward
  • Lizard stretch: 30 seconds each side
  • Downward dog to cobra flow: 5 repetitions
  • Repeat sequence 2-3 times

Intensity: You should be able to maintain a conversation

Duration: 25-30 minutes

Day 5: Strength Training - Lower Body (Knee-Friendly) (25-30 minutes)

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Bodyweight box squats (using chair): 10-12 reps
  • Glute bridges: 12-15 reps
  • Standing calf raises: 15-20 reps
  • Side-lying leg raises: 12-15 reps each side
  • Step-ups (low height): 10 each leg
  • Bird-dog: 10 each side

Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Complete 2-3 sets of each exercise:

  • Leg press (light weight, partial range): 10-12 reps
  • Hip abduction machine: 12-15 reps
  • Hip extension machine: 12-15 reps
  • Seated calf raises: 15-20 reps
  • Stability ball hamstring curls: 10-12 reps
  • Cable pull-throughs: 12-15 reps

Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Note: Focus on proper form and avoid deep knee flexion

Day 6: HIIT or Sprints (20-25 minutes)

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 6-8 rounds:

  • 30 seconds high intensity (jumping jacks, high knees, or mountain climbers)
  • 90 seconds recovery (walking or light movement)

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Warm up: 5 minutes light cardio

Complete 6-8 rounds on cardio machine of choice:

  • 30 seconds high intensity
  • 90 seconds recovery

Cool down: 5 minutes light movement and stretching

Day 7: Rest & Recovery

Complete rest or light activities:

  • Gentle walking
  • Light stretching
  • Yoga
  • Foam rolling

Focus on quality sleep and hydration

Notes for Week 1

  • Focus on learning proper form for all exercises
  • Modify any exercise that causes knee pain
  • The goal this week is to establish a consistent routine
  • All workouts are designed to be completed in 30 minutes or less
